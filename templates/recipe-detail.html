Lazy Egg Fried Rice 🍳

Difficulty: Easy 😌

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Soy Sauce
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add onion and cook
  3. Add egg and scramble
  4. Add rice and soy sauce
  5. Mix and serve hot 🍚
